# Competitor Snapshot

## Pet Nutrition (vs Hill's)
- **Royal Canin (Mars):** breed/condition-specific precision-nutrition positioning;
  very strong vet-channel relationships globally, including deeper APAC penetration
  than Hill's. Premium price, science-forward. Main threat in vet/specialty.
- **Purina Pro Plan (Nestlé):** broad availability (mass + specialty + Amazon),
  aggressive sports/performance and life-stage marketing, strong e-commerce and
  subscribe-and-save execution. Out-spends on paid social. Main threat on reach and
  e-commerce.

## Skin / Personal Care (vs Palmolive / Skin Health)
- **Dove (Unilever):** "real beauty" equity, moisturization credibility, huge mass
  distribution and share of voice. Benchmark for warmth + inclusivity messaging.
- **Olay (P&G):** anti-aging science positioning, strong mass-prestige bridge and
  retail media muscle. Benchmark for efficacy storytelling done compliantly.
- **CeraVe (L'Oréal):** derm-recommended, ingredient-led (ceramides), explosive
  social/UGC-driven growth and marketplace SEO. The insurgent playbook Skin Health
  brands have failed to match; dominant in exactly the digital-native space where
  Filorga/PCA Skin are weak.

## Positioning reads
- Hill's differentiator vs Royal Canin/Purina: therapeutic credibility + Youthful
  Vitality senior story; must not cede e-commerce/Amazon to Purina.
- Palmolive/Skin Health must study CeraVe's content velocity + marketplace SEO rather
  than compete on legacy prestige where CeraVe/Olay/Dove already win.
